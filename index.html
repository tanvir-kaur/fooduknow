Green Tea - a cup of good health !!!

Green tea is a rich source of polyphenols (catechins), which acts as antioxidants in the body and helps to fight against cancer. It also acts as a stimulant, just like the black tea, as it contains caffeine. The difference between these two these is the difference in how these are processed. Black tea undergoes a fermentation with the enzyme, polyphenol oxidase (PPO), and on the other hand, green tea undergoes steaming to prevent fermentation. It isn’t good to consume green tea along with the meals as it chelates iron and subsequently affects its absorption in the body. Green tea increases metabolism, and would help in us in staying fit and healthy by increasing the fat oxidation in the body. So next time, why not try a cup of health tea (green tea).

Immunity Building – First step to a Healthy Life

Mothers are always worried about the health of their children and this is the reason, we have to hear this dialogue so often, "don’t go out, it’s raining or else you will catch cold or fever". I think it’s true for almost everyone. Some people get sick quite often, while others don’t. Do you know why? It is all because of the immune system , which varies from individual to individual. Immunity is the power or ability of the body to fight the development of a disease, and if our immune system is weak, we tend to fall sick quite often. Hence, building a strong immunity is first step towards a healthy lifestyle.
