Immunity Building – First step to a Healthy Life

April 22,2017

Mothers are always worried about the health of their children and this is the reason, we have to hear this dialogue so often, "don’t go out, it’s raining or else you will catch cold or fever". I think it’s true for almost everyone. Some people get sick quite often, while others don’t. Do you know why?

It is all because of the immune system , which varies from individual to individual. Immunity is the power or ability of the body to fight the development of a disease, and if our immune system is weak, we tend to fall sick quite often. Hence, building a strong immunity is first step towards a healthy lifestyle.

Lactose Intolerance: A digestive disorder

April 22,2017

Do you always feel uneasy and suffer from digestive symptoms after having milk and milk products? Have you ever got suggestions from people around you like you might have a milk allergy? So lets know what this actually is.

You might think lactose intolerance is a type of milk allergy? No, that isnt correct. In milk allergy, immune response occurs due to milk proteins, while lactose intolerance is a digestive disorder. People with milk allergy cant even tolerate the minor level of milk proteins. On the other hand, lactose intolerants could tolerate up to 12 g of lactose per day and also, dairy products like hard cheese which are processed, henceforth containing less lactose as compared to milk.
